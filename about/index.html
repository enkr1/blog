About

Hello, World! I’m Pang Jing Hui 彭竞辉.

Welcome to the digital canvas where my dual passions for technology and creativity merge. As a seasoned Full-Stack Software Engineer, Technical Leader, and Project Manager based in the tech-savvy landscape of Singapore, I orchestrate symphonies of code that empower businesses and enrich lives.

My Story

Born from a curiosity that leaped beyond the conventional, my academic prowess was first acknowledged with 5As in SMKTPI, Malaysia. This led me to the shores of Singapore Polytechnic, where I not only honed my technical acumen in Data Structures and Algorithms but also refined my aesthetic senses in UI/UX Design. It’s here where I learned that the elegance of a user experience is crafted in the subtleties of code.

My Professional Palette

With a spectrum of expertise in languages like Elixir, Java, PHP, and a suite of modern frameworks and databases, I’ve shaped digital landscapes across diverse industries. My tenure at Pixium Digital saw me not just leading teams but architecting infrastructures and solutions that resonate with robustness and innovation.

From optimizing MySQL and PostgreSQL queries for lightning-fast analytics dashboards to pioneering a game-changing Zoom integration feature, my career is a testament to my belief that engineering is about overcoming the ‘impossible.’

My Ethos

I believe in the transformative power of technology – it’s a belief that has seen me through the meticulous crafting of advanced file management systems and responsive web applications that talk the language of efficiency and user-centricity.

In every project, I’ve embedded a part of my ethos: to enhance, to simplify, and to surpass expectations. My approach is to build not just for the present but for a future where my code continues to evolve and inspire.

Beyond the Brackets

But life isn’t all about algorithms and architectures. When the monitors dim, I step into the rhythm of life as a professional beatbox artist. It’s here, in the cadence of beats, that I find another dimension of creativity and expression. This harmony between my professional expertise and artistic passion is what makes me, me.

The Journey Continues

Every day, I write the future in code, driven by the same ‘Day 1’ spirit that has been my guiding star. Whether you’re here to explore a partnership, dive into my portfolio, or simply share in my journey, I’m glad you’re here.

Let’s connect the dots between technology and artistry. Let’s build something remarkable.
