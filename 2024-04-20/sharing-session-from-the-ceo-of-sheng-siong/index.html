Learning from Lim Hock Chee: Life and Business Insights from the CEO of Sheng Siong

On the evening of 19 April 2024, at 6:30 PM SGT, I had the privilege of attending a talk by Lim Hock Chee, the CEO of Sheng Siong, one of the leading supermarket chains in Singapore. This opportunity was made possible thanks to Troy Lai, a Personal Wealth Manager, who kindly invited me to this inspiring event. Speaking in Chinese, Mr. Lim shared profound insights on a range of topics from personal ethics to business strategies. This article reflects on those insights, exploring their significance and how they can be integrated into personal and professional life.

Key Takeaways from Lim Hock Chee’s Talk

1. The Importance of Integrity and Kindness (善良和正直的重要性)

Mr. Lim stressed that being kind and doing the right thing are fundamental to gaining respect and favour from others. This principle resonates deeply with me, as it aligns with my belief in the power of integrity as a cornerstone of personal and professional relationships.

Application to Life:
I aim to embody this principle by consistently making ethical choices in my daily interactions and business decisions, ensuring that my actions reflect my values.

2. Understanding Ourselves Before Others (先了解自己，再了解他人)

An intriguing aspect of Mr. Lim’s discussion was the emphasis on self-study over studying others. This introspective approach is crucial for personal growth and effective leadership.

Application to Life:
I plan to engage in regular self-reflection sessions to assess my strengths and areas for improvement, facilitating better personal development and enabling me to serve others more effectively.

3. Cultivating a Positive Work Culture (培养积极的工作文化)

Lim Hock Chee highlighted the creation of a positive company culture as essential for business success. He believes that leaders should lead by example to foster innovation and encourage their teams to embrace change.

Application to Life:
In my current role, I will strive to foster a collaborative and supportive environment, encouraging my team to innovate and propose solutions that challenge the status quo.

4. Entrepreneurship and Team Building (创业与团队建设)

The journey of entrepreneurship, as described by Mr. Lim, starts small with a clear vision and hard work. The emphasis on team building and nurturing talent was particularly poignant.

Application to Life:
Inspired by Mr. Lim, I am motivated to start a small side project that aligns with my passions. I will focus on building a small, dedicated team that shares my vision and values.

Why These Values Matter

These values imparted by Lim Hock Chee are not just business lessons; they are life lessons. They teach us about the kind of person we aspire to be and the kind of society we wish to create. By applying these principles, we not only enhance our own lives but also contribute positively to those around us.

Conclusion

The insights from Lim Hock Chee are timeless and universal. As I integrate these values into my life, I am reminded of the ripple effect that personal integrity and proactive leadership can have on a community and beyond. Sharing these lessons in both Chinese and English helps bridge cultural and linguistic gaps, bringing valuable lessons to a broader audience.
